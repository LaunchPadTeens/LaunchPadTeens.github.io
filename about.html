About Us

Launchpad Teens was born out of a shared desire to bridge the gap between students' potential and their future careers. We believe that with the right guidance and support, every teen can launch into a future where they are not only employable but thriving in what they love.

Why We Built It

Whether it’s helping students explore tech, medicine, design, or entrepreneurship—or giving innovators a space to pitch ideas—we are building the foundation for tomorrow's changemakers. We are a team of passionate developers and communicators committed to this mission.
